To Anti Corruption Commission 

We the uninvited companies and stakeholders operating in the Maldives tech and business community, write to formally express our concerns regarding the recent news about awarding of a public project with no open bidding 

These news has raised serious concerns and questions about the transparency, competitiveness and fairness of the bidding process linked to the project. Allegations and concerns relating to how the project was awarded risks undermining confidence in the integrity of the public procurement process.

A fair and transparent bidding process is fundamental to ensure equal opportunity for all businesses and safeguarding public funds 
Any news of preferential treatment or incompetence in carrying out a project from the awarded party can have long term consequences in investor trust and broader business environment in Maldives 

In light of these concerns we demand the ACC to 
- Initiate a comprehensive and independent investigation into the recent procurement and project awarding processes referenced in public reporting.
- Assess whether all relevant laws, regulations, and procurement guidelines have been properly followed.
- Ensure that any instances of misconduct, conflict of interest, or procedural violations are identified and addressed.
- Look into if the awarded party had any experience in app development. 
- Publicly communicate the findings of such an investigation in a transparent and timely manner

Our intentions is not to jump to any conclusions but to ensure that due process is upheld and that confidence in Maldives's procurement process remains strong 

We believe that a clear investigation will help reaffirm the principle of fairness, accountability and transparency that are essential for a healthy and competitive market 

with that said here is a list of allegations we would like to demand being cleared up
- was the bidding process invite only?
- did this project require an invite only bidding process
- was the project awarded to a family member of GM
- did the company who the project was awarded to have any experience in app development 

We appreciate your attention on this matter and trust that the commission will will take the appropriate steps necessary to address these concerns
